evangelion I recently finished Neon Genesis Evangelion and End of Evangelion, and I found it to be rather profound. To portray a diverse ranges of emotions, loneliness, sadness, depression, isolation, so raw. I think that I was able to find a bit of myself in each of the characters, which made watching it all the more enjoyable. I would highly recommend it to anyone who likes introspective and psychological animes, and also mechs.
